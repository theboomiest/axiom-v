The Nova Anchor was the primary starship meant to terraform the planet Axiom V. Attached to the Anchor were the companion ships Stellar Loom and Sky Hopper. 

The Loom carried several hundred individuals of varying species in cryo-sleep whom were meant to awaken and inhabit the planet after the Anchor had finished preliminary terraforming activities to make Axiom V more habitable.

Sky Hopper was stocked with backup materials and resources for the inhabitants of the Loom in case the primary stores on the Anchor were defective or unavailable.
