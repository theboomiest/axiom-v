Living, breathing, pulsating tunnels of organic mass. Teeth and claws reflexively rip and gouge at anything that passes by. Red rivers of blood pour from crevices above and coat yellow pustules and writhing cilia in ceaseless fluid. Baleful eyes open and shut from fleshy stalks, directing the flesh cave's antibodies to defend from foreign materials.

# Origin
The flesh caves are the unfortunate result of the [[Nutrient Goop]] from [[K3]] seeping through the cave systems and mixing with the runoff from the [[Nova Anchor]]'s damaged gene splicing machines.
