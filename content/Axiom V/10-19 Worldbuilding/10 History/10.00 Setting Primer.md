The arid scrubland that makes up [[Melako Valley]] is brutal and unforgiving. It barely sustains the few thousand lives that inhabit it. Unfortunately for them, the only worse place to live is anywhere else. 

A semicircle of mountainous badlands rings [[Melako Valley]] on 3 sides, protecting the valley's few settlements from the harsh sandstorms of the outer wastelands. 

The game takes place in [[Vaanchor]], a middling town of a few hundred, built in the shade of a curved, metallic structure that scrapes the sky above and gouges deep into the earth below.

Overland trade and travel between [[Vaanchor]] and the other settlements is infrequent, given the difficulty presented by the terrain and the danger posed by roving bands of [[14.03 Scrubland Raiders]]. Instead, [[14.01 Cave Runners]] make use of the extensive cave networks beneath [[Melako Valley]] to move goods and information between towns. This is still dangerous due to the aggressive creatures that make the underground their home, but usually safer than travel on the surface.

No faction in [[Melako Valley]] braves the underground quite like the [[14.02 Delvers]]. While [[14.01 Cave Runners]] stick as closely to the surface as they can, [[14.02 Delvers]] plunge deep into the subterranean hoping to find artifacts left behind by the [[Ancient Ones]]. Most do not survive their first descent.
