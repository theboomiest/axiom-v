The mysterious race that occupied [[Melako Valley]] before its current inhabitants. The extent of their influence over the rest of the world is unknown, but the artifacts they left behind indicate a culture far more technologically advanced than present civilization's. 
# Origin
The ancient ones were the progenitors of the current races, and resembled them very closely. They had spacefaring and terraforming technology and considered Axiom V a prime candidate for terraforming owing to its already breathable atmosphere. Something went wrong during the journey to Axiom V, resulting in a forced crash landing into a badlands region on the planet's surface. Coordinates for the crash site in the [[40.02 MLK]] coordinate system are M3L4K0.
# Starships
## Nova Anchor
The Nova Anchor was the primary starship meant to terraform the planet Axiom V. Attached to the Anchor were the companion ships Stellar Loom and Sky Hopper. 
## Stellar Loom
The Loom carried several hundred individuals of varying species in cryo-sleep whom were meant to awaken and inhabit the planet after the Anchor had finished preliminary terraforming activities to make Axiom V more habitable.
## Sky Hopper
Sky Hopper was stocked with backup materials and resources for the inhabitants of the Loom in case the primary stores on the Anchor were defective or unavailable.
