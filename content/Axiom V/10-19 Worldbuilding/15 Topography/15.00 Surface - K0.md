### Points of Interest
#### Last Light Saloon
The largest drinkery in town. The usual hangout for [[14.01 Cave Runners]] and [[14.02 Delvers]] who have news from the other settlements, which also means the locals go here first when they have something that needs doing underground.

The owner and proprietor, Dubya Dorne, is a small halfling woman who is quick to smile and laugh and ferocious with an iron frying pan. She takes her patrons' safety very seriously and "**won't suffer any of your foolery damnit, sit down and have a drink.**"
