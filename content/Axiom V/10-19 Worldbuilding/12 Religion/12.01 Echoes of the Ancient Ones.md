The only "official" religion in the valley, each of the three major settlements houses a church for the Echoes. Practitioners believe that the [[Ancient Ones]] were deific in their intelligence and therefore capable of predicting and pre-orchestrating all the conditions with which modern civilization contends. Echoists have faith that the Ancient Ones constructed a grand design to lead them toward utopia. Misfortune befalling the Echoists are trials left to test their faith or part of the Ancients' brilliance, avoiding some greater catastrophe to keep the faithful safe.
