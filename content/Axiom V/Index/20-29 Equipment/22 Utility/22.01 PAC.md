The Personal Atmospheric Condenser is a must for anyone seeking to leave a settlement. A miniature version of the Massive Aqua Condensers present in any town, the PAC straps onto a belt or harness and can provide enough potable water to sustain a humanoid until the internal battery is drained. PACs are named according to the number of days they can run before needing a recharge. The PAC-4, for example, can provide 4 days' worth of water for a humanoid before the battery dies.

Most [[14.02 Delvers]] permanently affix these to their backpacks to eliminate the confusing question: "Do you have your pack with you?"

PACs are artifacts from the ancient ones. No one knows how to create new PACs and they are rare and expensive as a result. Thankfully, they are nearly indestructible and can be recharged at any settlement's Massive Aqua Condenser.
