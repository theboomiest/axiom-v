# Biomes
## Mushroom Forest
### Creatures

#### Gloomshine Beetles
These beetles typically grow to be about a foot long; though [[14.02 Delvers]] claim to have seen beetles larger than a man scuttling about the deepest parts of the mushroom forest. Covered in a shiny, iridescent carapace and sporting venomous mandibles, these beetles have taken the lives of more [[14.01 Cave Runners]] than the runners would care to admit.

The gloomshine beetle consumes a strict diet of decaying mushrooms and anything else it can catch and they secrete a volatile fluid that can be used as a fuel source. As such, grease farmers pay top dollar for beetles brought back to the surface in good condition.
